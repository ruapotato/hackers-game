# Android and IOS Contender | Librem 5

It's about time.

An introduction to the Librem 5, a privacy-focused smartphone running PureOS (a GNU/Linux distribution). The Librem 5 represents a significant step toward offering users a genuine alternative to the Android and iOS duopoly, with a focus on user freedom and privacy.

## First Look at the Dev Kit

This video showcases the Librem 5 development kit, including:

- **Initial boot and startup** - First power-on experience
- **USB-C hub connectivity** - Expanding the phone's capabilities
- **USB ports and peripherals** - Using standard hardware with the phone
- **Linux environment** - Running a full GNU/Linux system on mobile hardware

## Why the Librem 5 Matters

The Librem 5 is designed with hardware features that prioritize user privacy and control:

- Hardware kill switches for camera, microphone, and cellular
- Open source software stack
- No reliance on proprietary Google or Apple services
- Full desktop Linux compatibility

As a contender in the smartphone market, the Librem 5 offers something fundamentally different from the mainstream options - true user ownership and control over their device.
