# Next Gen Linux Phones

Out with the old and in with the new! 2025 will be really hot for mobile Linux.

In this video, we take a look at the exciting new Linux phone options coming in 2025. The mobile Linux ecosystem is evolving rapidly, and there are several compelling devices on the horizon.

## Devices Covered

- **FLX1** from FuriLabs - A promising new entry in the Linux phone market
- **uConsole** from Clockwork Pi - A unique modular handheld device
- **Liberux** - An interesting project in the Linux phone space
- **Mecha Comet** - A new contender in mobile Linux
- **Jolla** - Continuing their journey with Sailfish OS

2025 is shaping up to be an exciting year for those interested in running Linux on their mobile devices.
