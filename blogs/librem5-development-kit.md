# librem5 development kit

Playing with the Librem5 development kit.

In this video, we take a hands-on look at the Librem 5 development kit from Purism. This early hardware gives us a glimpse into what the future of Linux smartphones could look like, exploring the capabilities and potential of this privacy-focused mobile device.

This video captures the dev kit at a specific point in the Librem 5's development journey:

- Software features available at the time

## The Bigger Picture

The Librem 5 development kit represented:

- A concrete step toward a privacy-focused smartphone
- Proof that mobile Linux was becoming viable
- An opportunity for community involvement in development
- Hope for those seeking alternatives to Android and iOS

The dev kit allowed enthusiasts and developers to experience the future of Linux phones before the production hardware was ready.
