Exploring cheat codes for open world gameplay in Ocarina of Time.

This video demonstrates some fun cheat codes for The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time that enable open world exploration, bypassing the normal game progression.

Cheat Codes

These are GameShark/Action Replay style codes for N64 emulators or original hardware with a cheat device.

Hold down 'L' to Levitate


D01C84B5 0020
811DAA90 40CB

Hold the L button to float upward, allowing you to reach areas normally inaccessible.

Hold down 'C-stick Right' to Run Fast


D01C84B5 0001
801DB258 0041

Press C-right to move at high speed, great for quickly traversing the world.

Hold down 'C-stick Right + L' to Run/Levitate


D01C84B5 0021
801DB258 0041
D01C84B5 0021
811DAA90 40CB

Combine both abilities - hold C-right and L together to fly around the world at high speed.

What You Can Do

With these codes enabled, you can:

  • Explore Hyrule freely from the start

  • Reach areas out of sequence

  • Discover hidden spots and boundaries

  • See the game world from new perspectives

  • Have fun breaking the intended progression

How to Use

1. Use an N64 emulator with cheat support (Project64, RetroArch, etc.)
2. Or use a GameShark/Action Replay on original hardware
3. Enter the codes in the cheat menu
4. Enable during gameplay
